The New York Times bestselling how-to and why-to guide to throwing out the old tools and methods for success and replacing them with a whole new way of living. Tim Ferriss has spent years learning the secrets of the "New Rich" — a fast-growing subculture that has abandoned the deferred-life plan and instead mastered the new currencies of time and mobility to create luxury lifestyles in the present.
